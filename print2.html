Print design portfolio

Most of my print work was done during the 1990s in New York. I worked full-time for The Sloan Group, a small boutique design firm with cients ranging from entertainment to financial companies. Later, I freelanced for a few years, mostly doing production work or filling in for absent employees. For a few months in 2004, I worked as a print designer at Johnson and Wales University.
